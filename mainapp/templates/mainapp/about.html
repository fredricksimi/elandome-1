About ELAN Dome Consultants

{% blocktrans %}ELAN (Education, Learning and Networking) Dome Consultants is an Africa based, bred and youth-led social & corporate outsourcing consultancy firm that strives to reshape the path of social service and international business engagements for students, individuals and institutions interested in integrating international experience and community service by offering high-quality international volunteer and service-learning engagements. {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}Additionally, ELAN Dome Consultants offers supervision and implementation support services, organizational programming, project management, external quality assurance checks, curriculum research and development and international students collaboration projects. Our aim is to enhance impactful initiatives in Africa by promoting cultural exchange, reliable investment in projects and holistic learning for local citizens in the diaspora and the international community.{% endblocktrans %}

Our Team of Experts

Awino Okore

{% trans "Managing Director and Principal Consultant Partnerships and Corporate Affairs" %}

{% blocktrans %}Awino is a seasoned Operations Lead with over five (5) years' experience in fast-paced multi-sectoral organisations across multiple countries. She has excelled in providing strategic, quality assurance, administrative, logistical, coordination and implementation support to high-level executives and project teams with multiple tasks. She facilitates human centred design thinking and challenge driven education workshops for faculty, corporate staff and university students.{% endblocktrans %}
